Civillian Operations

Mission Statement

The Civilian Operations department is the foundation of Roleplay. The mission of the Civilian Department is to create fun and realistic roleplay scenarios so the community will have hours of enjoyment for both the emergency services and the fellow civilians. It is our goal at Club Lux to make sure that the civilians act appropriately and professionally at all times.

Required Traits

  • Respect others with sensitivity and possess interpersonal skills
  • Make decisions with a clear judgement
  • Work efficiently and as a team
  • Remain mature consistently and be able to respect discipline
  • Be honest, respect integrity, and others
  • Fluently speak and write using the English language
  • Follow rules and regulations

Think you have what it takes?

Civillian Operations is always looking for individuals dedicated to providing creative and realistic roleplay scenarios.
