Mission Statement

We aim to protect the lives, property and rights of citizens of Blaine County to maintain order and law. The Blaine County Sheriff’s Office strives to accomplish this mission by setting goals and objectives and practicing the core values of the Office. It is our mission to improve and maintain the quality of life we enjoy and to ensure that our county is a safe place to live, work and visit. We will continue to be dedicated to this mission, while serving the County. The Blaine County Sheriff’s Office’s values are its principles and beliefs, which guide the Office’s members as they pursue the Office’s mission.

Required Traits

  • Respect others with sensitivity and possess interpersonal skills
  • Make decisions with a clear judgement
  • Work efficiently and as a team
  • Remain mature consistently and be able to respect discipline
  • Be honest, respect integrity, and others
  • Fluently speak and write using the English language
