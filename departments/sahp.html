Mission Statement

It is the mission of the San Andreas Highway Patrol & Department of Public Safety to provide the highest level of safety, service, and integrity to the people of San Andreas. This is accomplished through our dedicated members of the department. Our department members make it our goal to prevent injuries and loss of life, control traffic and emergency situations, protect public and private assets and continue to improve functionality and efficiency.

Bureaus

In the San Andreas Highway Patrol we have specialized bureaus that cover a multitude of different situations throughout the State of San Andreas. These include:

Air Support Unit (ASU)

Motor Bike Unit (MBU)

Tactical Enforcement Bureau (TEB)

Commercial Vehicle Enforcement (CVE)

Motor Recovery Unit (MRU)

Required Traits

  • Respect others with sensitivity and possess interpersonal skills
  • Make decisions with a clear judgement
  • Work efficiently and as a team
  • Remain mature consistently and be able to respect discipline
  • Be honest, respect integrity, and others
  • Fluently speak and write using the English language
  • Follow rules and regulations
